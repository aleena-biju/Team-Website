Who we are

We were 9 individuals who came from different background having different opinions, different understanding and different perceptions grouping together to play as a single team. This helps us in understanding

  • Different ideas to make a presentstion.
  • The time management as a team should work.
  • How we could be positive.
  • The dedication we initiate.
  • How to communicate each other so to increase confidence and communication skill.

The most important lesson we have learned is motivation which could helps us getting more involved in activities. We were also able to identify our hidden talents and took a step forward to break the chain we were locked in. We got many chances to showcase ourselves and we were able to do the best of it. We could also help our collegues in their difficult times which shows our team spirit.
